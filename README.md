# Applied Harm & Crime Prevention Portfolio

**Modeling Coercion, Exploitation, and Violence ‚Äî and Where Intervention Is Possible**

---

## üìå Portfolio Overview

This portfolio is a modular, applied research program focused on **understanding and preventing coercive harm**.  
It models how grievance, ideology, sexual exploitation, and financial incentives evolve into real-world violence and organized crime.

Rather than studying isolated crimes, the portfolio treats harm as a **system**:

- how people are recruited
    
- how control is established
    
- how exploitation scales
    
- how violence emerges
    
- how systems fail
    
- and where prevention can be inserted
    

The portfolio consists of **five interlinked projects**, each addressing a different layer of the same harm ecosystem.

Together, they produce a **single integrated source of truth** about:

- coercive pipelines
    
- exploitation dynamics
    
- grievance-to-violence pathways
    
- financial flows
    
- and prevention strategies
    

---

## üéØ Portfolio Goals

- Model coercion and exploitation as structured processes
    
- Identify early warning signals and escalation points
    
- Translate behavioral analysis into prevention logic
    
- Demonstrate applied reasoning suitable for Trust & Safety, NGOs, and security research
    
- Produce structured, reusable analytical artifacts
    

---
